About BridgeX

BridgeX is a simulated Web3 cross-chain bridge platform designed to demonstrate how digital assets move across different blockchain networks.

It allows users to select different cryptocurrencies and simulate bridging them across networks with real-time visual feedback and animations.

The platform focuses on user experience, transparency, and intuitive design to replicate how modern decentralized finance (DeFi) applications work.

BridgeX is not connected to real blockchain networks and uses mock data to provide a safe and interactive learning experience.
